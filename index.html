### Welcome to WeOn Software.

WeOn is a open source software that offers free wifi in the public bus. which have the following features:
* Analyze data and generate information on urban mobility and transport.
* Two-way communication between passengers and the transport provider.
* Generate solutions for public transport.
* Generate service surveys.
* Offer free Internet to passengers.
